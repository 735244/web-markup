    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

    1. What makes Dietology different from other diet programs?
    At Dietology, we offer personalized nutrition plans based on cutting-edge science, including nutrigenomics and microbiome analysis. Our approach is tailored to your unique needs and designed for long-term results.
    2. Can I change my subscription plan later?
    Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time to better suit your needs. Simply contact our support team for assistance.
    3. Does insurance cover Dietology’s services?
    We work with many insurance providers to make our services accessible. Please check with your provider to confirm coverage or reach out to us for assistance.
    4. How are the meal plans personalized?
    Our plans are crafted using information about your health, lifestyle, and goals. For advanced plans, we include insights from genetic and microbiome analyses to create the most effective recommendations for you.
    5. Can I cancel my subscription?
    Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time with no hidden fees. However, we’d love to help you find the right plan to meet your needs before making any changes.
